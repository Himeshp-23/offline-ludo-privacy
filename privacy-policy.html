Privacy Policy

Offline Ludo Multiplayer

Effective date: May 31, 2026

Overview

Offline Ludo Multiplayer is a local multiplayer game developed by Himesh Pant. It is designed for nearby players connected to the same Wi-Fi network or phone hotspot. The app does not use an operated online server, create user accounts, show ads, or include analytics.

Information stored on your phone

The app stores your display name, audio preferences, tutorial completion, and chat-rules acceptance on your phone. This information is used only to remember your choices. App backup is disabled. You can delete this information by clearing the app's storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Information shared during a local game

When you join a room, your display name, lobby chat messages, and game actions are sent over the local network to the host phone. The host relays the information needed to keep the nearby players synchronized. Local IP addresses are used to discover rooms and create the connection. The app does not upload or retain this information on an operated internet server. Room information disappears when the room closes.

Lobby chat and moderation

Lobby chat is visible to the nearby players in the room. Messages and display names are filtered locally. Players can mute another player, and a report sent during a room allows the host phone to remove that player. Reports are handled inside the local room and are not uploaded or stored after the room closes.

Permissions

The app uses network and Wi-Fi access to find and connect to games on your local network. On newer Android versions it asks for Nearby Devices permission for this local connection flow. It also uses vibration for gameplay feedback. These permissions are not used for advertising, analytics, or location tracking.

Third parties

The app does not include advertising, analytics, account, or cloud-storage SDKs. Data is not sold or shared with a developer-operated third-party service.

Security and your choices

Only join rooms created by people you trust and use a trusted local network. You may avoid lobby chat, change your display name, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app at any time.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Himesh Pant using the developer contact email published on the Offline Ludo Multiplayer Google Play listing.
